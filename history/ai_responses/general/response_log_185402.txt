Hrithik Roshan is a well-known Indian film actor. He is the son of filmmaker Rakesh Roshan and has appeared in numerous Bollywood films, showcasing his versatility as a performer. Some of his notable works include 'Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai', 'Krrish', 'Dhoom 2', and 'Jodhaa Akbar', among others. He is widely regarded for his dancing skills and has received several awards for his acting.
