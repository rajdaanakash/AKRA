Virat Kohli is a renowned Indian international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian cricket team in all three international formats. Born in 1988, he is known for his exceptional batting skills and has established himself as one of the most dependable and successful batsmen in the world.

Kohli has had a highly successful career, having captained India to the 2008 Under-19 World Cup title and winning several ICC trophies with the senior team. He is a world champion multiple times over and has earned a reputation for his fiery attitude and remarkable maturity on the field.

Throughout his career, Kohli has consistently demonstrated his impressive batting skills, earning him numerous awards and recognition. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of his generation, and his achievements have made him a household name in the world of cricket.
